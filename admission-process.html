Admission Process

Home Admission Process

Step 1: Fill Enquiry Form

Parents will fill the Enquiry Form on our website. Click here to fill enquiry form.

Step 2: Receive Communication

Upon submission, parents will receive details regarding the next steps.

Step 3: Counselling & Visit

Our counsellor will connect with parents to share school information, fee structure, and schedule a campus visit.

Step 4: We Confirm Admission

After receiving required documents, parents will get a confirmation message or call.

Admission Requirements

  • Original previous year’s progress report
  • Original transfer or leaving certificate
  • Copy of child’s birth certificate
  • Copy of Aadhar card (for Indian citizens)
  • Passport size photos of child and parents
